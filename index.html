Beyond Phishing
Phishing is just one part of social engineering — a larger scheme to manipulate people using psychology.
Impersonating IT or HR
Attackers may call pretending to be IT support or message about payroll changes and performance reviews.
Physical & Digital Deception
They may show up pretending to deliver a package or hack via USB, or send emails pushing your buttons.
Tricky Messages
Emails like “New org chart – see who’s moving where!”, “Security update: verify credentials now”, or “You’re being offboarded – click for details” are designed to make you react without thinking.
